Climate Data Timeseries

These graphs plot statistics for temperatures during the specified month, at the specified location, over the years since temperature recording began at the location.

Two sets of temperatures are plotted. The maximum temperatures recorded during the month, and the minimum temperatures recorded during the month (green and brown, respectively).

The statistics included are:

  • Rolling Means: The mean of the maximum or minimum temperature for the month over the past ten years (blue and light grey, respectively).
  • Rolling Highest: The highest maximum or minimum reached for the month in the past ten years (red and purple, respectively).
  • Rolling Lowest: The lowest maximum or minimum reached for the month in the past ten years (aqua and dark grey, respectively).

Mouse over the lines to find which statistic they represent.
